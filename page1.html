Among Us

(click on Among Us title to fadeToggle)

A guide for beginners and tips & tricks


Play with 4-10 players and go on a spaceship together as a crew but beware as one
or more random players among the Crew are Impostors bent on killing everyone!
Crewmates don't know who imposters are and imposters try to kill everyone without
being caught. Crewmates and imposters can sway other crewmates to vote people
out during meetings if they are suspicious enough.

How to win as crewmate:

(click on Crewmate Screen image to show how to win list)
  • complete tasks
  • Report any suspicious crewmates
  • Always try to fix sabotages

(click on Imposter Screen image to show how to win list)

How to win as Imposter:

  • Sabotage tasks
  • kill as many crewmates as you can without getting caught
  • Fake tasks
  • Lie during meetings
  • Don't look suspicious
