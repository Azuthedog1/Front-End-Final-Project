A Guide to the Seasons


Tips and tricks about Spring

On the first day:

  • Plant lots of beans since they take a while to grow but keep producing all season long. Save at least one for later.
  • Plant lots of parsnips since they grow quickly and are cheap. You also need to save five gold tier parsnips and one normal one.
  • Plant and save at least one potato.
  • Plant and save at least one cauliflower.
